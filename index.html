Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How do I join?+

Simply select a membership tier above, click the Join button, and complete the enrollment form. Our team will process your registration and send your welcome pack within 3–5 business days.

Is membership annual?+

Yes, all memberships are billed annually. You will receive a renewal reminder 30 days before your membership expires, with the option to continue or upgrade your tier.

Can benefits be transferred?+

Membership benefits are non-transferable and are tied to the registered member. However, certain event tickets purchased through your membership may be used by guests you designate.

Are events guaranteed?+

Events are subject to availability and scheduling. Premium and VIP Elite members receive priority access, significantly increasing their likelihood of attending. We communicate all event details well in advance.

How do I receive my membership card?+

Physical membership cards and credentials are shipped to your registered delivery address within 7–10 business days of successful enrollment. You will also receive a digital version immediately upon confirmation.
